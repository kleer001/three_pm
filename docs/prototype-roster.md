# Y2K Roguelike — Genre Character Roster

A playful companion to the [`neo_cyber_music`](./README.md) engine: if each of the nine
electronic genres were a playable character in an action-adventure roguelike set in a
**California high school in the year 2000** (dial-up, JNCO jeans, Discmans, Surge soda,
Y2K dread), who would they be?

Each character's personality, clique, and signature **move** channels their genre — and
the gimmicks map back to how that genre actually behaves in the engine: techno loops and
repeats, trance builds-and-drops, dubtechno is minimal/stealthy, ambient heals through
stillness, psytrance rolls fast and relentless. Three options per genre, 27 total.

> Brainstormed for fun (a Haiku sub-agent). Not a build spec — a vibe board. The genres
> and their musical character are real (see the engine); the high-schoolers are not.

---

## 1. Techno — *hypnotic, driving, machine-like*

- **AJAX** — "I optimize everything, even my sleep schedule."
  *Computer-lab wiz / early adopter.* **Precision Strike**: repeats the same combo in an
  endless loop, +1 damage each cycle until he breaks rhythm. *Y2K:* ironic Napster tee;
  47 browser tabs open at all times.
- **Cassandra** — "Repetition isn't boring, it's meditative."
  *Raver girl / underground-club regular.* **Hypnotic Momentum**: the longer she fights
  one target without switching, the faster her attack speed scales. *Y2K:* trades CDs at
  lunch; worn copy of Underworld's *Dubnobasswithmyheadman*.
- **Marcus "Tick" Rodriguez** — "Four-on-the-floor, always."
  *Drummer in three bands.* **Metronome Fortress**: a defensive rhythm that reflects
  damage on exact beat intervals. *Y2K:* makes beats on a Casio VL-Tone between classes.

## 2. Trance — *euphoric, uplifting, builds & drops*

- **Skyler** — "Every fight is a 12-minute journey to enlightenment."
  *Scene kid / spiritual wanderer.* **The Build**: stacks a power meter over turns;
  release for a devastating stun finisher. *Y2K:* glow sticks year-round; Paul van Dyk
  lyrics on her binder.
- **Dimitri** — "Intensity isn't an option, it's a guarantee."
  *Mysterious exchange student.* **Euphoric Ascension**: each hit grants brief
  invulnerability as he climbs to peak damage. *Y2K:* perfect trance-lyric memory; AOL
  status permanently "Away."
- **Jessica "Jess" Chen** — "I live for the drop."
  *Popular girl / secret clubkid.* **Emotional Release**: sacrifice HP for a massive AOE
  that scales with damage taken. *Y2K:* burns mixes onto TDK CD-Rs for her BMW's changer.

## 3. Industrial — *harsh, distorted, aggressive*

- **Razor** — "Brutal efficiency disguised as controlled chaos."
  *Goth/industrial kid.* **Metal Shred**: attacks ignore armor and apply a lingering
  "corroded" debuff. *Y2K:* draws NIN logos on homework.
- **Diesel** — "Pain is just data to process."
  *Shop-class dropout / hot-rod head.* **Overclock**: voluntarily take damage to spike
  attack power for one turn. *Y2K:* modded his Walkman past safe volume; Skinny Puppy
  sticker on his locker.
- **Maya Steele** — "I break things for a living. And I love it."
  *Skateboarder / DIY punk.* **Deconstruction**: each hit shatters the enemy's best gear,
  forcing them to fight weaker. *Y2K:* hand-screens her own noise-band tees, $3 at shows.

## 4. House — *warm, groovy, soulful*

- **Andre** — "Everything's better with a smooth groove and good friends."
  *Basketball captain / heart of the team.* **Good Vibes Aura**: heals nearby allies
  while dealing steady damage; stronger with more allies near. *Y2K:* DJ booth in his
  basement.
- **Venus** — "I'm the rhythm your body didn't know it needed."
  *Drama-club star / dancer.* **Soulful Sway**: each dodge triggers a charming
  counterattack that lowers enemy attack. *Y2K:* practices voguing in gym class.
- **Tony Groove** — "Love and house music are the only laws I recognize."
  *Record-store philosopher.* **Communal Healing**: restores HP based on clean combos
  landed this fight. *Y2K:* quotes house lyrics as life advice.

## 5. Acid — *squelchy 303, psychedelic, weird*

- **ZigZag** — "Reality is just a filter sweep away from total weirdness."
  *Art-class gremlin / conspiracy theorist.* **303 Squelch**: warps enemy targeting so
  they hit their own allies. *Y2K:* doodles fractals; modulates a Speak & Spell for fun.
- **Prism** — "Perception is mutable. Use it."
  *Loner / zine publisher.* **Psychedelic Distortion**: scrambles all enemy stats for
  several turns. *Y2K:* MS Paint album art, scanned via Zip drive.
- **Neon Kid (Sam)** — "The weirdest path is always the fun one."
  *Skater gamer.* **Acid Trip**: wildly randomizes his own stats — gambling for +400% or
  −50%. *Y2K:* green-screen-modded Game Boy running acid palettes.

## 6. Dubtechno — *deep, dark, foggy, minimal*

- **Shadow** — "Patience and darkness are my only allies."
  *Quiet goth / late-night IRC lurker.* **Deep Fade**: turns partially invisible; attacks
  cost less but deal half damage until he re-emerges. *Y2K:* LiveJournal with 0 public
  entries.
- **Reeves** — "Every note is a whisper in an endless void."
  *Photographer / darkroom enthusiast.* **Minimal Precision**: devastatingly efficient
  hits; bonus damage if he hasn't moved in several turns. *Y2K:* black-and-white photos
  of abandoned malls.
- **Echo** — "I am the space between the spaces."
  *Introvert / bedroom producer.* **Fog Bank**: persistent cover that drops everyone's
  accuracy but his. *Y2K:* records dubs on a 4-track Tascam; the tape hiss is intentional.

## 7. Psytrance — *fast, rolling, alien, intense*

- **Quantum** — "Reality folds when I'm around. Try to keep up."
  *Math-competition champion / chaos theorist.* **Hyperdrive Spin**: triple-speed attacks
  at reduced accuracy, each hit building momentum. *Y2K:* holographic spiral glasses,
  unironically.
- **Zephyr** — "I am the storm that tears the fabric of time."
  *Parkour enthusiast before parkour existed.* **Alien Surge**: rapid combos stack an
  "alien energy" buff to unleash. *Y2K:* alien stickers covering his JanSport.
- **Raven** — "My mind moves faster than physics allows."
  *Debate-team ace.* **Rolling Thunder**: chained attacks grant multiplicative damage +
  a dodge boost. *Y2K:* No-Doz and max-volume Infected Mushroom.

## 8. Ambient — *calm, dreamy, spacious*

- **Luna** — "Healing happens in silence and stillness."
  *Library aide / meditation enthusiast.* **Serene Presence**: the longer she stands
  still, the more damage she absorbs for nearby allies. *Y2K:* holistic-healing crystal
  necklace; a "Study/Healing" CD-R.
- **Whisper** — "I am the guardian of peaceful spaces."
  *Florist's kid / gentle soul.* **Bloom**: heals allies each turn of inaction and grants
  defense buffs nearby. *Y2K:* pressed flowers in her yearbook for the whole class.
- **Jasper Drift** — "Time slows when you stop fighting it."
  *Philosophy nerd / tai-chi practitioner.* **Drift State**: slows all combat timers —
  effects last longer, but he acts slower too. *Y2K:* MiniDisc collection of "rain on
  different surfaces."

## 9. Synthwave — *retro, neon, nostalgic, 80s throwback*

- **Chrome** — "The 80s never ended; I'm living proof."
  *VHS collector / arcade champ.* **Neon Burst**: stylized combos apply a "retro glow"
  that multiplies next turn's damage. *Y2K:* Walkman with "the good batteries"; every
  John Hughes film on tape.
- **Synth** — "Neon lights, synth bleeps, and infinite cool."
  *Tech geek / CRT hoarder.* **Retro Flash**: a wave of neon blinds enemies and resets
  their attack patterns. *Y2K:* neon-modded custom PC running synthwave screensavers 24/7.
- **Valentine** — "Nostalgia is my superpower."
  *Fashion plate / thrift regular.* **Stylish Nostalgia**: each hit triggers a flashback
  that re-deals last turn's damage. *Y2K:* fanny pack, a Tamagotchi, and a laserdisc
  remote as accessories.
