A · BARS

Labelled bars (2×2)

Four labelled meters in a 2×2 block. The balanced default — quick to compare across heroes, doesn't shout. Closest to the in-game HUD.

B · EQUALISER

Mini equaliser

Stats as a tiny vertical EQ in the corner. The most on-theme — every card looks like a channel strip. Visual, less precise to read exact values.

C · NUMERIC

Mono readout

Plain “SPD 5 · CON 5 · STR 4 · MAG 7”. Most legible and unambiguous; the data-forward tDR option. Least decorative.

D · BIG NUMERALS

Big colour numerals

Four large numbers in the hero's colour, tiny labels under. Boldest and most poster-like — the stat IS the graphic. Chad's 8/8 really pops.
