# 3pm — Relationships

The salient edges between the nine Merriton kids. Deliberately *not* a full
9×9 matrix — only the relationships that surface in copy. Each new member's intro
activates **one** existing relationship (an "entry triangle"): newcomer + a
referenced kid + Marvin as the witness/hub. No romance in this group (0%); a
"crush" exists only as one-sided, unspoken, never-acted frustration.

## The edges

**Spine (the contamination):**
- **Wendolyn → Jess** — fear + anger → guilt. Burned her the cursed CD to take
  the popular girl down a peg; caught it herself; now owes the apology.

**Athletes:**
- **Dash ↔ Chad** — rivalry + grudging respect. Sprinter vs wrestling captain,
  forever arguing who'd last alone.

**The two who "called it" (and can't stand each other's reason):**
- **Eugene ↔ Wendolyn** — wary respect. Rationalist and occultist both knew
  something was wrong; neither can stomach the other's explanation.
- **ZigZag ↔ Wendolyn** — frenemy. Both claim they saw the truth first (the
  simulation vs the veil).

**Friction:**
- **ZigZag → Eugene** — paranoia ("you built the thing watching us");
  **Eugene → ZigZag** — contempt that cracks when the sensors act wrong.
- **Dash → Jasper** — maddened by his stillness; **Jasper → Dash** — serene
  indifference.

**Aesthetes:**
- **Valentine ↔ Jess** — allies, thread of status-comparison.
- **Valentine → Wendolyn** — respect for committing to a whole look.

**The one crush (one-sided, unspoken, never acted):**
- **ZigZag → Valentine** — loses his conspiracy swagger, fumbles, deflects.
  Valentine is oblivious and kind. Never becomes anything.

**The two anchors:**
- **Marvin** — the switchboard. Everyone's casual friend, nobody's best friend;
  the normal axis the others are measured against. Faintly intimidated by all.
- **Jasper** — the still point. Non-entangled; the one they vent *at*.
