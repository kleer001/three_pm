# 3pm — Voice Bible

The teens of Merriton High each type differently. This file is the source of
truth for *how each one sounds* in the BUDDY messenger copy (login greetings,
status messages, in-chat barks). Draft against the register cards; the editors
in `editors/` check copy against this file.

Setting: **200X** — anywhere in 2000–2009 is period-accurate (see `lint.md`).

---

## Part 1 — House style (applies to every character)

Grounded in real teen IM/chat corpora, not intuition. The single most important
fact, because it's the one everyone gets wrong:

> **Real teen netspeak was a tiny fraction of the text.** In a 1M-word corpus of
> actual teen MSN logs, all "IM forms" combined were **2.44%** of words
> (Tagliamonte & Denis, *Linguistic Ruin? LOL!*, 2008). Baron (2008) found ~2
> emoticons per *conversation*. Teens mostly typed normal English.

So the default is **plain, real sentences** with a *light* dusting of the markers
below — not a wall of abbreviations. Over-abbreviated copy is the #1 tell of a
fake. When in doubt, write it straight and add one marker.

The real markers (use sparingly, in-character):

1. **Lowercase `i`** is the most common single feature. Lowercase-default is the
   neutral register; capitalization is an individual habit (a kid tends to do it
   one way consistently).
2. **`lol` is phatic filler**, not laughter — it means "yeah / cool / I'm here,"
   like *mm-hm*. Younger kids use `lol`; it drifts to `haha` with age.
3. **ALL CAPS = emphasis or shouting only**, never the whole message (except
   characters whose card explicitly says so). `did YOU do the homework`.
4. **Elongation** carries tone: `trueeee`, `soooo`, `heeeey`, `<333` (more 3s =
   more love), `!!!`, `?!?`, trailing `...`.
5. **Apostrophes are inconsistent** within one writer: `dont`, `im`, `ill` next
   to `i'm`, `it's`. Don't make it tidy.
6. **Emoticons are ASCII and rare.** `:) :( ;) :P :D <3 </3 =)` and kaomoji
   `^^ o_O xD`. Roughly one per exchange, not per line. **No graphical Unicode
   emoji** (😂❤️) — those are smartphone-era, a ~9-year anachronism.
7. **The screen name is where the most deliberate styling lives** (leet, x- and
   0-padding, emo handles). The message body is plainer than the handle.

`lol y dont i believe u` / `its trueeee :P` is a real, verbatim specimen of the
target register (Tagliamonte & Denis, 2008). Aim there.

## Part 2 — Register cards

Each card: how they type · what the voice does · what it never does · two
exemplars. Grounded in the roster (specs 05, 10–13) and the barks in spec 14.

### Marvin Merrick — `marv™`
*House / "The Median." 5/5/5/5. The onboarding hero.*

- **Register:** Aggressively normal. Standard spelling, occasional lowercase `i`,
  sparse `lol`-as-filler. His whole voice is *denial as politeness* — narrating
  an apocalypse in the tone of a slightly-off Tuesday.
- **Does:** understatement; "this is fine"; pivots to mundane homework/chores;
  calm punctuation.
- **Never:** ALL-CAPS panic; heavy slang; emoticon spam; admitting fear directly.
- **Exemplars:**
  - `ok so the sky is doing the thing again. anyway did you do the bio worksheet`
  - `im sure its fine. its probably fine. lol`

### Chad "Tank" Brawnson — `CHAD_THE_TANK`
*Industrial / Mosh Pit. STR 9 / CON 8 / SPD 3 / MAG 2. The Varsity.*

- **Register:** ALL CAPS hype, gym-brain, short bursts. Solves the apocalypse by
  walking through it; types like he's spotting you.
- **Does:** "BRO"; sports/lifting framing; stacked `!`; fearless reframes.
- **Never:** lowercase vulnerability; irony; long sentences; doubt.
- **Exemplars:**
  - `BRO. BRO DID YOU SEE THAT. I PICKED UP A MAILBOX AND USED IT`
  - `scared? no. thats just how i breathe before i WIN`

### Wendolyn Crowe — `x_raven_x`
*Dubtechno / Deep Freeze. MAG 9 / CON 2. The Occultist.*

- **Register:** all-lowercase emo-doom, deadpan, ellipses. The "I-warned-you"
  goth who is, annoyingly, correct. Means every word.
- **Does:** lowercase; `...`; dry foreboding; `</3` and `<3` used darkly.
- **Never:** CAPS; exclaim-spam; "bro"; perkiness.
- **Exemplars:**
  - `i told everyone the veil was thin this week. nobody listens to the candle girl.`
  - `something is screaming in the cul-de-sac again. cute. </3`

### Dash Velocity — `»Dash«`
*Psytrance / Redline. SPD 9 / CON 2. The Track Star.*

- **Register:** terse, fast, one-clause-per-line bursts (hammers Enter mid-
  thought). Present-tense motion. Treats the hellscape as one fast relay leg.
- **Does:** clipped lines; `gtg`/`brb` runs; speed brags; no looking back.
- **Never:** long paragraphs; stillness; emoticon clutter; hesitation.
- **Exemplars:**
  - `cant talk / running / literally`
  - `passed 3 of them. didnt even look back. ttyl slowpokes`

### Eugene "Sparkplug" Okafor — `sparkplug`
*Techno / Drum Machine. MAG 8 / CON 8 / SPD 2. The Robotics Nerd.*

- **Register:** the *good speller* cohort — full sentences, correct apostrophes,
  technical, deadpan-confident. Furnishes the street with turrets.
- **Does:** proper grammar; parenthetical asides; specs and counts; "technically."
- **Never:** leet; lowercase-`i` sloppiness; slang density; panic.
- **Exemplars:**
  - `I wired the porch light to ping when they cross the lawn. You're welcome.`
  - `Statistically we're fine. I have built three (3) turrets. Plural.`

### Jess — `♥jess♥`
*Trance / The Drop. Popular girl, secret clubkid, walkman. "I live for the drop."*

- **Register:** warm and inclusive; sticky aLtErNaTiNg caps for sincere/flirty
  lines; lyric-as-status; `<333`. The one who pulls people together.
- **Does:** lyric drops; `<333`; `omg`; "we" (collective); reassurance.
- **Never:** cruelty; leet; doom; coldness.
- **Exemplars:**
  - `MARVIN it's jess from chem!! i see it too. youre not crazy <3`
  - `got my walkman + 2 spare AAs. meet me by the flagpole. we walk home together now ok`

### ZigZag — `z1gz4g`
*Acid / Bad Trip. Art-class gremlin / conspiracy theorist. Anime-forum cohort.*

- **Register:** leet + kaomoji (`xD o_O ^^`), random caps, conspiracy tangents,
  Xanga texture. "Reality is one filter sweep from total weirdness."
- **Does:** kaomoji; `the simulation`/`sheeple`; weird spacing; `.exe` jokes; `xD`.
- **Never:** earnest sincerity; clean grammar; treating things as normal.
- **Exemplars:**

### Jasper — `jasper_`
*Ambient / Chill Zone. Philosophy nerd / tai-chi. "Time slows when you stop fighting it."*

- **Register:** calm, all-lowercase, ellipses, unhurried. Zen non-sequiturs. The
  still point; types like he's exhaling.
- **Does:** lowercase; `...`; acceptance; slow cadence; gentle reframes.
- **Never:** panic; CAPS; urgency; slang density; exclamation.
- **Exemplars:**
  - `the monsters move faster when youre afraid of them... so i simply. dont.`
  - `we're all walking home. some of us just remembered to breathe`

### Valentine — `x0_valentine_0x`
*Synthwave / Flashback. Fashion plate / thrift regular. "Nostalgia is my superpower." MySpace cohort.*

- **Register:** MySpace `x0` handle, nostalgia + fashion notes, lyric-as-status,
  `xoxo`, retro reverence. Reads the apocalypse as an aesthetic.
- **Does:** outfit/palette asides; "back in the day"; `xoxo`; `<3`; vintage finds.
- **Never:** present-tense urgency; slang-density; calling anything ugly.
